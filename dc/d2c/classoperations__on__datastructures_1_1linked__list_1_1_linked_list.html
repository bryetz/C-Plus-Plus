Finds middle node of a linked list.

An algorithm using a slow and fast pointer, where the fast pointer is twice as fast as the slow pointer. When the fast pointer reaches the end of the doubly linked list, the slow pointer will be at the middle.

Returns
A pointer to the middle node.
